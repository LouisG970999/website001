Privacy controls

Your data choices

Clear local app records, understand what leaves your device, or request help with submitted feedback.

Local-first by default Most app records remain in the browser on the device where they were created.

Delete local data

Open TechSpec Scanner, go to Settings, then use the relevant data-management action to clear scan history, known parts, preferences, test records, or all local app data.

Open app settings

Export before deleting

You can export reports, known-parts records, test logs, or a local backup before clearing data. Keep exports only where you trust the device and storage location.

Photos and AI analysis

Photos and context are transmitted only when you start an analysis. They pass through the TechSpec Scanner backend to Google Gemini. The current TechSpec Scanner backend does not create a permanent photo library from scan uploads.

Feedback deletion request

Beta feedback may be stored on the hosted server. To request deletion, email support@techspecscanner.com and include the feedback ID, approximate submission date, and any contact email entered in the form.

No account required

The current beta does not require a TechSpec Scanner account and does not provide cloud synchronization. If accounts or cloud storage are introduced, this page and the privacy policy will be updated before release.

Questions

For a privacy or data request, contact support@techspecscanner.com.
