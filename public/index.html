AI and privacy acknowledgement

TechSpec Scanner is an AI-assisted tool. Review these points before using it with real components.

  • AI results can be incomplete or incorrect and must be verified with measurements, markings, manufacturer documentation, and engineering judgment.
  • Photos and scan context you submit are sent through the backend to Google Gemini for analysis.
  • Do not use the result as the sole basis for safety-critical, purchasing, repair, or design decisions.
  • Do not submit API keys, passwords, private documents, or unrelated personal data.
